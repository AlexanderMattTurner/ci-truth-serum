- `check-pinned-downloads` now also flags a Dockerfile `ADD <url> <dest>` that fetches an `http(s)://` artifact without a nearby checksum/signature check — the idiomatic Dockerfile download was previously invisible to the lint even though it already ran on Dockerfiles. Docker's own `ADD --checksum=sha256:<digest>` counts as the verification, and the existing `# pin-exempt: <reason>` opt-out applies.
