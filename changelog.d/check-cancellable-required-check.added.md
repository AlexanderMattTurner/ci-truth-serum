- `check-cancellable-required-check` (Tier 2, opt-in): flags a workflow that declares a required check (`# required-check: true` marker) AND carries a **static** (ref-less) workflow-level `concurrency.group` with a truthy `cancel-in-progress`. A sibling ref then cancels the running job — and its workflow-level `always()` reporter — wholesale, so the reporter never posts and the required check hangs at "Expected — Waiting" forever. This is the residual class the static-concurrency lint misses: it keys off the explicit required-check marker, catching marker-based required checks that have no decide-gate heuristic. Per-ref groups and non-cancellable groups pass. Opt out with `# cancellable-required-check-ok`.
