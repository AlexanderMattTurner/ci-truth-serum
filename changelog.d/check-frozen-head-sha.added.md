- `check-frozen-head-sha` (Tier 1, honesty): flags any workflow or composite-action step whose `run:` script or a `with:` input value references `github.event.pull_request.head.sha`. That SHA is frozen at trigger time, so a force-push or an autofix `--amend` after the trigger makes it point at a diverged commit — a diff-range step then silently mis-scopes to the whole branch, and a checkout lands a stale tree. Derive the head from the checkout (`git rev-parse HEAD`) instead. `base.sha`/`head.ref` never match. Opt a genuine lease-pinning use out with `# frozen-head-ok: <reason>` in the step's source block; `env:`-routed uses are out of scope.
